Inequality between Opportunities for college students.

Every year nearly 1 million international students choose the United States as a location to obtain higher learning, and each year these learning centers seem to charge these students small fortunes[1]. Universities around the country typically do not offer the same amount of finacial opportunities to these students as they do the local population. One could argue that this is likely due to the fact that university want to give back to their community or residing state. That they would like to further the bond between the local people and the university.Although the main goal of a Univeristy is to equally inform and teach those who strive to excel and yearn to change the world for the better. This seemes to be an altruistic vision for some international students, yet we plan to change that.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to help fellow international undergraduate students to settle comfortably in U.S. while they go along with their busy student lives. Usually, their time is lost adjusting to their new life as adults and living in a new environment.

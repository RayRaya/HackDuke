Advice for International Students

As an International Student, it won't be easy for you to adapt to the cultural and social norms that regulate the lives of the local residents. Therefore, we have come up with a few commom problems faced by International Students and a few major points to be taken care of as you continue to work hard and attain your goals



Problems


  • Change in the observed social attitude, with special emphasis on the etiquette of an individual.
  • Expensive tuition fees, when compared to that available in your home country.
  • Scammers targeting the young and inexperienced students with false allegations and implications.
  • A more mature sense of responsibility expected from a young adult, such as yourself


Tips and Points

  1. Be careful about your passport, visa, and Form - I20. These are your most important means of identification.
  2. Make friends with all. Move beyond your comfort zone and network with anyone and everyone you meet.
  3. Create an account in an American Bank within the first few days of arrival. This will help you manage your
    financal statements easily.
  4. The most important tip - ENJOY!!! The next few years will be among the few most important time periods of
    your life. Make sure they are memorable
